Discover the Magic of Momo

Momo is a beloved Himalayan dumpling—steamed, stuffed, and served with spicy chutney or soup. Originating in Tibet and spreading through Nepal, Bhutan, and India, it's now a global comfort food icon.

Popular Types of Momo

  • Steamed Momo: Soft, juicy, and classic.
  • Fried Momo: Crispy and golden.
  • Jhol Momo: Served in a spicy soup made from chutney, lemon juice, salt, and chilies.
  • C-Momo: Tossed in fiery chili sauce.

Why People Love It

Whether you're warming up on a rainy day or craving bold flavors, momo delivers. It's a symbol of hospitality, a street food staple, and a dish that adapts to every mood—from traditional to fusion.
